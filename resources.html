In the development of this project, our team chose to focus on some of the big ethical issues that's happening in 2020. Below are some of the resources that we feel people should be aware of.

Low Income Housing

    Housing for low income families has become a huge issue in San Francisco. We decided to provide the community with some local housing resources that can point people in the right direction on finding housings.

  • SF Gov website for low income housing
  • Affordable Housing
  • Bay Area Housing

Careers

    Some low-income families in the bay area are in need of jobs and career options, especially in these times. These some are websites to apply for jobs, and internships for young people that can also teach about career exploration and job readiness.

  • Opportunities for All
  • SF Human Services
  • Google Careers for Students
  • MYEEP
  • SF YouthWorks

Food Banks

    These websites are some food banks around the bay area that are available, whether you are in need or helping the people in need.

  • SF-Marin Food Bank
  • St. Anthonys Foundation
