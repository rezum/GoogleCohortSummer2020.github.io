What We've Learned As Part of Google Cohort

Through the OFA internship program, the Google Tech Cohort interns were presented informational sessions on various projects, roles, and divisions currently found at Google. From learning about software development and business analytics to social impact projects and data centers, I gained both unexpected and valuable knowledge hearing from Google employees’ stories and advice. In one specific session on measurements and analytics, a Googler conducted an interactive session going through industry vocabulary, useful skills, and an analyst activity to highlight sample trends and problems regularly handled. Another session covered the communications and public affairs division at Google, where three Googlers shared details about the importance of their work, their day-to-day work routines, and the challenges they face. It was intriguing to hear startups under Google and Googlers’ side-projects come to life and make an impact. I looked forward to each session as each one was a different and unique topic!

- Yolanda

I learned a lot during these sessions with Google. I use Google everyday, and I always wondered, what goes behind making Google? Everytime I search something, it brings up millions of results. Attending these Google sessions helps me understand how Google works. Workers also share out their personal stories of how they ended up at Google and how they chose their career which inspires me to work hard.

- Damond

The Google session that stuck out the most to me was the google.org session. I learned how much Google was helping the communities that they were a part of by providing support for local charities and fundraisers. Furthermore, I liked the fact that Google is pushing to support people with dealing with social issues by helping spread awareness through their sites like Google.org.

- Meshach

The Google sessions this summer have been a real learning experience for me, especially the sessions with developers and engineers from different divisions in Google. It is my interest as a computer science student to enter the industry where Google is one of the leading figures. I enjoyed learning and discussing the career path and experience within Google, and I appreciated all the time and efforts from the google employees that came out and discussed their role and experience with all of us. I learned a lot of what it means to be a Googler and had a better understanding of what I need to do in order to proceed in my own career.
